ASSUMPTIONS & OPEN QUESTIONS

  • Vertical-specific LAL approach (Option C): Recommended over the combined-only approach. Costs 15 extra minutes of Ads Manager work for better Meta optimization signal on Auto ALC + Home ALC.
  • Source data assumed correct: Trusting Anthony Kaylor's campaign-ID mapping. If the IDs include non-Mil.com data anywhere, the suppression list could over-suppress legitimate prospects. Spot-check a few rows from each campaign ID to confirm they look like Mil.com customers.
  • No phone field = lower match rate ceiling: Without phone, expect match rates in the 55-75% range. Anthony's offer to add phone if rates are low is the escape hatch.

  • Recipients-only data: The data is QW accepted+sold leads. We don't have the "form submitter but didn't get accepted by QW" cohort, which is the larger user base. Lauren's 30-day cooldown list would fill this gap.
