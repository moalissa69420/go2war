Why we built the Kudu.
A
AlphaStyle Founder
Toronto, Canada
"I wanted a jacket that could actually handle a Canadian winter without making me look like I was dressed for a bunker. Nothing existed at this price. So we built it."
The Kudu wasn't designed as a fashion piece. It started as an engineering problem — a double hood for real wind, down insulation that holds shape, drape that doesn't collapse when you sit. We made 200. They sold in a week. We made 500 more. Same result.
That's the whole story. We build the things that should already exist, at the price they should already cost.
