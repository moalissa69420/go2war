Fee Structure Decision

⚠️ CRITICAL DISCOVERY: Initial 1% Fee Was Untenable

Polymarket US charges 0.01%. The initial proposed 1% fee would have been 100x more expensive. High-volume traders would never migrate to a 100x cost increase. We pivoted immediately.

Competitive Fee Comparison

Platform Trading Fee Withdrawal Other Fees Effective Cost
Polymarket (Global) 0% ~$0.50-2 Gas only ~0%
Polymarket (US) 0.01% ~$0.50-2 Gas only ~0.01%
Drift BET 0.1% Minimal FUEL rewards offset ~0.05-0.1%
Kalshi 1-4% $0 None ~1-4%
Predica (Final) 0.1-0.25% Minimal Token rewards offset ~0.1-0.25%

❌ At 1% Fees

  • 100x more expensive than Polymarket US
  • High-volume traders avoid platform
  • Only viable for retail with no alternatives
  • Cannot compete on volume metrics
  • Market makers demand higher spreads

✅ At 0.1-0.25% Fees

  • 10-25x Polymarket US (still premium)
  • Competitive with Drift BET
  • Offset with token rewards
  • Attracts volume-focused traders
  • Sustainable market maker economics

  • Week 1-4: $50K+/week subsidies
  • Month 2-3: 3-5 market maker partnerships
  • Month 3-6: Organic liquidity growth
  • Month 6+: Self-sustaining pools

Token Launch Strategy

Option A: Beat Polymarket

  • Launch before Q1 2026
  • First-mover in Solana predictions
  • Risk: Less time to build community

Option B: Differentiate

  • Launch Q2 2026 with superior tokenomics
  • Learn from POLY launch
  • Risk: Polymarket captures mindshare

Our Decision: We chose to differentiate with superior tokenomics, learning from Polymarket's POLY launch while building a stronger community foundation.

The crypto-native vertical dominance approach proved correct. By avoiding sports markets and focusing on crypto predictions, we positioned Predica in a less contested space with better retention characteristics.

Key Insight: The fee structure pivot was the most critical decision—the original 1% proposal would have made the platform uncompetitive.

Strategic Window: Establishing market presence before Polymarket US launch was essential for building community and liquidity moats.
