Brand Guidelines

INTRODUCTION

Internet history that never asked for permission.

$TROLL is the only meme coin with exclusive worldwide intellectual property rights to the Trollface — the internet's oldest and most culturally significant meme, created in 2008 by Carlos Ramirez.

These guidelines ensure consistent, premium representation of the brand across all touchpoints.

BRAND ESSENCE

Confidence earned through time, repetition, and survival across eras.

This is not merch that asks for attention. It assumes relevance.

BRAND VALUES

Confident

Unbothered

Timeless

Predates every platform. Outlasts every trend.

LOGO SYSTEM

Clear Space

Maintain minimum clear space around the logo equal to the height of the letter "U" in the wordmark. This ensures the logo remains legible and uncluttered.

U U U U

Minimum clear space = height of "U"

LOGO SYSTEM

Logo Misuse

Protect the integrity of the logo. Never alter, distort, or compromise its appearance.

Don't stretch or distort

Don't rotate

Don't use low contrast
