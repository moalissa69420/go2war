The TROLL Origin Story

The Creation of Trollface

Date: September 19, 2008
Creator: Carlos Ramirez, 18-year-old Oakland college student ("Whynne")
Platform: DeviantArt
Tool: Microsoft Paint

"That's what that whole little trollface comic was about—trolling as an excuse for being an idiot."
— Carlos Ramirez on the original intent

Viral Evolution Timeline

2008 Created September 19; posted to DeviantArt as part of webcomic satirizing 4chan trolling
2009 Explosive spread across 4chan, Reddit (/r/f7u12), Tumblr. First Urban Dictionary definition.
2010 Ramirez registers with U.S. Copyright Office (July 27, 2010)
2010-2012 Golden era of rage comics; Trollface becomes universal symbol
2011-2015 Commercial licensing generates $100,000+ (Hot Topic, games, merchandise)
2020 Revival through "Trollge" variants and "Cover Yourself in Oil" memes
May 2024 $TROLL token launches on Pump.fun
April 2025 TROLL explodes 174,948% to $42M market cap (4/20 rally)
August 2025 Six-figure IP licensing deal signed with Carlos Ramirez. ATH of $283.8M.
December 2025 Current: ~$33M market cap, 44,000+ holders
