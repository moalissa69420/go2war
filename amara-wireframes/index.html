Developer Notes — Product Page

  • Gallery: Left column is position: sticky; top: 80px; — stays visible while user scrolls through info
  • Thumbnails: Click to swap main image. Video thumbnail shows play icon overlay.
  • Variants: Sharp-edged selectors. Active state: border: 2px solid #940707
  • Add to Bag: Button text updates dynamically with selected variant price
  • Subscription Toggle: Show for consumable products (Water Jelly, Wipes, Shimmer Dust). Use ReCharge or Shopify Subscriptions.
  • Trust Signals: Always visible below Add to Bag — discreet shipping is critical
  • Accordions: Expand/collapse with max-height transition. First one open by default.
  • Reviews: Judge.me integration. Enable photo reviews. Show photo grid above individual reviews.
  • Mobile: Gallery becomes horizontal swipe carousel. Sticky "Add to Bag" bar at bottom.

Developer Notes — Mobile

  • Breakpoints: Mobile: 0-767px · Tablet: 768-1023px · Desktop: 1024px+
  • Touch Targets: All interactive elements minimum 44px × 44px
  • Sticky Add to Bag: Fixed bottom bar on PDP with price + CTA. z-index: 100
  • Gallery: Horizontal swipe with dot pagination. Use Flickity or Swiper.js
  • Menu: Full-screen overlay, slide in from left. transition: transform 0.3s ease
  • Cart Drawer: Slide in from right. Includes upsell products below items.
  • Safe Areas: Respect iOS safe areas for Dynamic Island and home indicator
